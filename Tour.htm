Gangtok to Lachen

Day 03: Gangtok to Lachen Early morning, after breakfast, check out from the hotel and start your ride towards Lachen a hidden hamlet located in the North Sikkim. During your journey from Gangtok to Lachen, on this journey, you will stop by picturesque, scenic destinations Lachen which is situated 8800 feet above sea level. On the way, you will get the chance to visit beautiful destinations such as Seven Sisters Water Falls, Singhik View point, and Naga Water Falls. You will reach Lachen by late evening, you will spend the night at a hotel here.

Lachen to Gurudongmar

DAY 4: From Lachen to Gurudongmar Lake and Chopta Valley to Lachung On the 4th day, you will visit the land of Yaks, blue ship and reach Lachung by the eveningAfter an early morning breakfast, our representatives will drive you to Gurudongmar Lake, popularly known as the sacred lake. It is surrounded by snow-capped mountains. The sight of this abode is one that you’d cherish forever. After a session of unwinding here, you will visit Chopta Valley and Thangu. You will then head back to the hotel for lunch and start your journey to Lachung. Once you reach Lachung, you can spend the evening visiting the popular monasteries here.


Lachung to Yumthang

DAY 5: From Lachung drive to Yumthang and then head back to Gangtok On the 5th day, you will visit the Yumthang valley, popularly known as the valley of flowers. On this day, you will also get the chance to experience the beautiful waterfalls on the way back to Gangtok. The day starts early on Day 5. Our representative will drive you to the Valley of Flowers. Yumthang is also popular as the summer grazing ground of the Yaks. After ample time here, you will journey back to Lachung, on the way you will visit Hot Spring. You will then get back at the hotel for lunch and depart for Gangtok. On this journey, you will stop by the beautiful Bheema & Twin Falls. You will reach Gangtok by evening, check in to the hotel and take some rest. An extra tour to Zero can be arranged on this day at extra costs.


Gangtok to Darjeeling

DAY 6: From Gangtok to Darjeeling Before the end of the beautiful tour, a journey to the final destination, DarjeelingPost Breakfast, our representative will drive you to Darjeeling. Once you reach Darjeeling, check in at the hotel at Darjeeling and prepare for yourself for some amazing sleep amid the chilly weather.

Explore the nooks and corners of Darjeeling

DAY 7: Explore the nooks and corners of Darjeeling The final tour before the trip ends. A tour to the view-point, monasteries and tea gardens of DarjeelingThe weather may push you to sleep more but resist that temptation and get up on your feet at 4 am to experience the panoramic view from Tiger hill situated 2950 m above sea level. From here, you can see the snow-capped Mt. Kanchenjunga. On the way back from Tiger hill, you will stop at Ghoom Monastery and Batasia Loop. You will then get back to the hotel for lunch and head out for a local sightseeing. This tour includes a visit to Chitrey Tea Garden, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute, Tenzing Rock, Peace Pagoda, Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park and Japanese Temple. In the evening do spend time shopping for gifts and relishing street side delicacies at Chowrasta and Mall Road.

Darjeeling back to New Jalpaiguri

DAY 8: From Darjeeling back to New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (NJP)/ Bagdogra Airport(IXB) (88 km/3 hrs drive) The 8th day, the day it all comes to an end. By now, your body may be tired because of all the travelling but your heart craves for more. But you have memories umpteen, so with those happy lifetime memories head back Bagdodra Airport.
